# Superstore Europe Tableau Analysis

## Project Overview
This project was developed for **Superstore Europe** to support a more data-driven decision-making process through interactive visual analytics in Tableau.  
The goal was to replace static analysis methods with a dynamic solution able to monitor business performance across Europe and support strategic marketing decisions.

## Business Context
Superstore wanted a system to continuously monitor its European operations and identify the products with the highest growth potential.  
The project was designed to help the business move from traditional spreadsheets and static presentations to a more interactive and efficient analytical environment.

## Project Objectives
The project had two main objectives:

### 1. Interactive Tableau Dashboard
Create a dashboard to monitor key business metrics such as:
- sales trend over time
- profit by country
- quantity of products shipped by shipping class

The dashboard was designed to help users explore performance through dynamic filters and interactive visualizations.

### 2. Tableau Story for Marketing Budget Allocation
Create a Tableau Story to support marketing budget decisions by identifying:
- products that deserve **more budget**
- products that deserve **less budget**
- products that should be **removed** from the catalog

The analytical logic was based on comparing product sales performance and profit margins, with the aim of identifying high-potential but underperforming products.

## Dataset
The dataset contains business data related to Superstore operations in Europe, including information useful for sales, profit, shipping, and product-level analysis.

Main analytical dimensions include:
- order and sales information
- country and city
- shipping class
- product performance
- profit measures

## Dashboard Features
The Tableau dashboard was built to provide an interactive overview of business performance.

Main features:
- **Sales over time** to identify trends and seasonality
- **Profit by country** to compare profitability across European markets
- **Shipped quantity by shipping class** to evaluate logistics distribution
- **Dynamic filters** for country, time period, shipping class, and product
- **Interactive charts** to allow flexible exploration of the data

## Tableau Story
The Tableau Story was designed to guide strategic decisions on marketing budget allocation.

The analysis focused on:
- products with high margin but low sales
- products with low profitability
- products generating limited return compared to their cost

The story structure follows a business logic based on:
- **Context**
- **Conflict**
- **Conclusion**

This approach helps communicate insights clearly and supports practical decision-making.
